Tater Tot Casserole Recipe

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 35 minutes | Serves: 6-8 people

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 2 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 bag of onion seasoning
  • 1 lb of sour cream
  • About 81 frozen tater tots
  • 2 cups of shredded cheese

Cookware

  • 1 oven
  • 1 skillet
  • 1 meat chopper
  • 4-8 paper towel sheets
  • 1 9x13 inch baking pan
  • 1 timer

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F.

Step 2

Acquire a skillet and spray it with a fine coat of cooking spray.

Step 3

Turn the stove on medium-high heat.

Step 4

Acquire 1 lb of ground beef and place it in the skillet.

Step 5

Acquire another 1 lb of ground beef and place it in the skillet as well.

Step 6

Use a meat chopper to mash the ground beef into small pieces.

Step 7

Continue mashing and stirring the meat until it is fully browned.

Step 8

(Optional) Use paper towels to soak up the grease from the cooked meat.

Step 9

Sprinkle a packet of onion seasoning throughout the meat.

Step 10

Add 1 lb of sour cream to the pan and stir until it is fully combined with the meat.

Step 11

Acquire a 9×13 inch baking pan.

Step 12

Pour the contents of the skillet into the baking pan.

Step 13

Spread the ground beef evenly throughout the pan.

Step 14

Acquire a bag of tater tots (about 81).

Step 15

Place the tater tots evenly on top of the meat to create a full layer.

Step 16

Acquire an oven mitt.

Step 17

Place the casserole pan in the oven and bake for 30 minutes. Remove when done.

Step 18

Acquire 2 cups of shredded cheese.

Step 19

Spread the cheese over the casserole until the top is mostly covered.

Step 20

Place the casserole back in the oven and cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 21

Retrieve your finished tater tot casserole and let cool.

Step 22

Serve and enjoy!
